Show Accept/Cancel Bar

If on, a red/green vertical bar is shown in the editor, as soon as the text is modified. a click on the green area will "accept", the red will "cancel" your changes.

Show Search Bar in Editor

If on, the fields to specify text-searches are shown at the bottom of the browser as a bar (like in the fireFox internet browser). If off, a dialog box pops up.

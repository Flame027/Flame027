Menu

undo last move

Undo the last move

undo all moves

Undo all moves (animated) - has the same effect as reset

reset

Reset the cube (like ``Undo all moves'', but not animated)

scramble

Scramble the cube randomly

Mouse operation

rotating the cube

Press the left mouse button, and move the mouse. This rotates the cube horizontally or vertically.

manipulating the cube

Press the middle mouse button on one of the center subCubes to rotate that plane clockwise.
If the "Shift" key is also pressed, the plane is rotated counter clockwise.

Keyboard commands

frontView

Press "f" to reset the cubes rotation for a front view.

backView

Press "b" to reset the cubes rotation for a back view.
